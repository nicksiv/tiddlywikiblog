20230210 So vast the music...

10th February 2023 at 10:19pm

Interesting lists for today that will keep me busy for a long time (and that's if I won't get bored).

The first one is from the great https://rateyourmusic.com site which I'm very fond of. Best album in every music genre: There are 2.078 genres with the best album for each. So far (I'm on page 4) I can testify that yes, there are that many genres. A bit over analytic to split music in so many sub-genres but a little browsing will prove that the music anyone listens to is such a small fragment of what's really going on out there!

The second one is a cool database of jazz records The Jazzomat Research Project which contains 456 records including every little piece of info you can imagine including various stats, chords and even music transcription.

Both of those lists really blew my mind and I much respect the people who put work into this! üôè
